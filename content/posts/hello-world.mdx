This is the first post on the new site. Keeping it minimal on purpose: one page, a short about, a few links, and a running log of what I'm building and thinking about.

## Why this space

I spend a lot of time on the internet. Most of that time I'm making things that live there. Having a place that's just mine, without an algorithm in between, feels important.

## How it works

New posts live as plain MDX files. I write, commit, and deploy. No CMS, no database, no friction.

## What's next

I want to use this as a thinking space more than a journal. Short essays on design, on AI, on running a brand solo. We'll see what sticks.
